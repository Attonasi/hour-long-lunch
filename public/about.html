About the App

Hour Long Lunch emerged as an idea to find lunch around Codefellows when we only have an hour to eat. Within that hour for lunch, part of that time is spent walking, part is spent waiting in line, and much less time to actually eat. Hour Lunch Lunch maps food truck locations around South Lake Union for every day of the week.
